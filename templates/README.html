README

Currently the model can only recognise a limited number of anime characters due to limited dataset and model training time.

The list of currently supported characters and their anime are :

Kosaki Onodera and Kirisaki Chitoge from Nisekoi

Saber and Tohsaka Rin from Fate/Stay Night

Asuka Langley Soryu and Rei Ayanami from Neon Evangelion

Kurumi Tokisaki and Tobichii Origami from Date-A-Live

This makes it 8 characters from 4 different anime.
